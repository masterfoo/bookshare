About

This site allows Fitchburg State students and alumni to borrow and share old textbooks.

Our intention is that users will share books without any sort of monetary compensation just because its a super nice thing to do.

Feel free to browse available books and if you find something you need make an account, using your Fitchburg State email address, and send them a borrow request. If they accept your request you can decide on a place, preferably on campus, to exchange the book.

With your account you can also pay it forward and share a textbook with others as well.
